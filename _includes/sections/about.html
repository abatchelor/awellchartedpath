A Well Charted Path

We are two people who spent a year traveling a well charted path.

We traveled to 33 countries across 6 continents over 11 months.

After a couple of fits and starts we ended up planning this trip for about 5 years before we actually took it and we have a Google Sheets database full of content developed over those years. We have detailed budgets, itineraries, and lots of lessons learned that we'll share here; as well all the details of what we did along the way.
